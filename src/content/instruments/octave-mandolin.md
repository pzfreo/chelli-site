An Octave Mandolin of my own design inspired by the work of Graham McDonald. Guitar bodied with an archtop and soundholes inspired by 1950s instruments. This mandolin has a lovely resonant tone and great playability. Fully bound ebony fretboard with brass fretmarkers, inlaid headstock. The neck design is fully detachable for easy resets. Indian Rosewood back and sides, Spanish Cedar neck and carved spruce front. All solid woods.
